House Arrest

Overview

House Arrest brings a new approach to internet-usage regulation while providing valuable parental control features. House arrest uses aninternet-regulation approach
which is unlike any other application currently available to smartphone users!
Many apps have been introduced which attempt to solve internet usage issues pertaining to parental control, addiction and productivity, pornography and harmful sites,
parental controls, and child internet safety. However, one can download every internet regulation app in the app store and find that each suffers from major pitfalls
and loopholes which render them innefective. Our team has set out to implement internet regulation in a new way!
The House Arrest app implements specific smartphone functionality restrictions corresponding to the time, GPS location, and/or the network being utilized by the user.
The user will be able to set personal restrictions for themselves and to team up with a buddy or parent who can help enforce restrictions.

Upon the installation of the smartphone app, a user will be able to restrict:
  • Apps on the device
  • URL's
  • the Internet as a whole
  • Specific networks

This functionality will empower numerous use cases. Struggling with social media overuse while at work? Utilize House Arrest's location-aware restrictions.
Don't want your child roaming the internet while away from your home? Utilize House Arrest's network-aware restrictions.
Struggling with pornography? Team up with a buddy and utilize House Arrest's effective tools to keep you away from unwanted websites.
